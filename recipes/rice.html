Yellow Rice

Recipes

Description

This yellow rice recipe is simple to make and super delicious! It turns out beautifully golden and very fragrant. While yellow rice is often made with saffron, it can easily be made with turmeric instead, a spice which has incredible health benefits. Yellow rice can be found across a number of culinary traditions, including many in Thailand and India, and is a wonderful companion for curries of all kinds, seafood, fish, and chicken dishes. This also makes a nice side dish for any meal—a perfect potluck addition.
