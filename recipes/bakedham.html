Rita's Sweet Holiday Baked Ham

Rita's Culinary Bliss: A Sweet Holiday Baked Ham Delight

Savor the essence of festive indulgence with Rita's Sweet Holiday Baked Ham, a culinary masterpiece promising sweetness and sophistication in every flavorful bite.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup maple syrup
  • 1 cup orange juice
  • 1 cup ginger ale
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • 1 box round wooden toothpicks
  • ½ cup honey
  • 1 (10 ounce) jar maraschino cherries, halved
  • 1 (12 pound) fully-cooked, bone-in ham
  • 1 large heat-resistant nylon oven bag (such as Reynolds Oven Bags)
  • 1 (15.25 ounce) can pineapple slices in juice, drained

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Mix maple syrup, orange juice, ginger ale, brown sugar, and honey together in a medium bowl. Stir in juice from maraschino cherries, and 1/2 of the cherries.
  3. Score the outer edge of the ham with a sharp knife in a diamond pattern. Cuts should be about 1/4 inch deep to allow the ham to soak up the juice.
  4. Place ham into an oven bag, and carefully pour the juice mixture over it, keeping all of the juice in the bag (it may look like there is too much juice, but the ham will soak it up while baking).
  5. Place pineapple slices on top of ham, and secure with toothpicks. Place cherries into the center of pineapple rings, and secure with toothpicks also. Tie the end of the bag closed with the ties provided, place in a large roasting pan, and cut a few small slits in the top of the oven bag.
  6. Bake ham for 2 hours in the preheated oven. The internal temperature should be 140 degrees F (60 degrees C) when done. Be sure not to touch the bone when taking the temperature.
  7. Remove ham from bag to a serving plate, and let stand for 10 minutes before carving.
